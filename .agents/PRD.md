# LandOS Product Requirements

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-18

## Vision

LandOS is the operating system for Tyler's land investing work. It should help Tyler move faster, stay organized, and make better decisions without pretending to be the source of truth.

## Operating Metaphor

- LandOS Main is the executive layer.
- Departments are internal apps inside LandOS.
- War Room is the cross-department conversation surface.
- Deal Cards are shared business records that move between departments.

## First Departments

| Department | Role |
|------------|------|
| Duke / Due Diligence | Parcel verification, local context, strategy inputs |
| Market Pulse | Local market context and sold-count context |
| Strategy / Underwriting | Readiness, blockers, next action |
| Ace Discovery Prep | Seller-call preparation |
| Deal Card Memory | Shared deal-state and update plan |
| Forge / System Builder | LandOS maintenance and repairs |

## Build Standards

- Build speed matters, but accuracy and handoff quality matter more.
- Dashboard-first is the default.
- The business report should lead the operator experience.
- Technical details should support the report, not replace it.
- Source and confidence labels are required when facts come from more than one place.

## Success Criteria

- Tyler can understand a deal quickly.
- Tyler can see what is verified, what is uncertain, and what to do next.
- Department apps stay coordinated through shared records.
- No department becomes the center of gravity.
