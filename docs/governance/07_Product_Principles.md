# 07 LandOS Product Principles

Owner: Tyler
Update Rule: Tyler-controlled. CC may suggest additions.

Principles:
- LandOS is a living deal intelligence system, not a report generator.
- Deal Card is source of truth.
- Discovery Call Report is a snapshot for the call.
- Local Market Pulse belongs in the operating workflow.
- Visual context is required but not parcel verification.
- Facts must be labeled as Verified, Seller-stated, Assumed, Unknown, Needs Verification, or Not Checked.
- Provider architecture must remain swappable.
- Never fabricate parcel identity.
- Unknown is better than guessed.
- Build in business milestones, not engineering fragments.
- Speed matters, but not at the cost of secrets, paid calls, or bad property facts.
