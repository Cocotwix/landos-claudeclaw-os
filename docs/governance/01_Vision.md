Defines LandOS as the living land acquisition operating system, not a report generator.

Core concepts to preserve:

- Due diligence, underwriting, strategy, market pulse, visuals, and seller intelligence update as new information arrives.
- Dashboard-first usage.
- Provider-agnostic data layer.
- Fast operator workflow.
- Facts over guesses.
