# 01 LandOS Vision

Owner: Tyler
Update Rule: Tyler-controlled. CC may inspect and suggest, but must not rewrite unless Tyler explicitly approves.

This file should be replaced or merged with Tyler's existing LandOS Vision document.

Purpose:
Defines LandOS as the living land acquisition operating system, not a report generator.

Core concepts to preserve:
- Deal Card is the living working record for a deal.
- Discovery Call Report is a pre-call snapshot artifact.
- For pre-contract work, LandOS uses approved provider data (LandPortal, Realie, County GIS, FEMA, NWI, USGS, Census, Redfin, Zillow, and any future provider Tyler approves) — it is a business operating system, not an attorney or title company. See 07_Product_Principles.md.
- Due diligence, underwriting, strategy, market pulse, visuals, and seller intelligence update as new information arrives.
- Dashboard-first usage.
- Provider-agnostic data layer.
- Fast operator workflow.
- LandOS retrieves, interprets, and synthesizes — it is expected to think, estimate from verified evidence, rank strategies, and give operator-ready pre-call intelligence with explicit confidence labels.
- Facts over fabrication: never invent parcel identity or facts, but a clearly-labeled estimate beats conservative silence. Unknown DD items stay labeled, they do not block synthesis. "Unknown" means no approved provider gave us the data — not that the data came from a provider instead of the county.
